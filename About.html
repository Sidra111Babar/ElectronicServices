Who is Fori Hal Company?

Fori Hal Company is Pakistan’s leading online marketplace that connects service providers with customers in an easy and secure way. Through our modern website, we help customers and vendors connect more efficiently, reliably, and affordably, offering them a better lifestyle.

Our Empowerment Mission

Our goal is to help thousands of local technicians find reliable earning opportunities for a good livelihood. They register with us and get training and support from the CS department, which make order-taking and order-completion easier for them. Through the implementation all SOPs, we make them the 1st choice of service seekers, enabling them to earn better and live with integrity.

Our Achievements

Launched as Fori Hal, the company has grown rapidly in the past three years. We’ve expanded our services, reached more areas, and seen strong growth in app downloads and website traffic.

Customer Growth: We have 50,000 monthly visitors to our website, and 100,000 active users.
